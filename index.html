About SafeEats

SafeEats was created to help people with food allergies and dietary restrictions make informed decisions about the products they consume. Our mission is to provide accurate, real-time information about food ingredients and allergens through an easy-to-use mobile application.

Whether you're managing celiac disease, nut allergies, or following a specific diet, SafeEats gives you the confidence to shop and eat safely.
