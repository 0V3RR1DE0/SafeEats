Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SafeEats is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Profile Data: Dietary restrictions and allergen preferences you voluntarily enter
  • Scan History: Products you scan, stored locally on your device
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve our service
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to fix bugs and improve stability

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide personalized allergen alerts based on your dietary restrictions
  • Maintain your scan history for easy reference
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your privacy and data security:

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Your profiles and scan history are not uploaded to our servers
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

SafeEats integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenFoodFacts: Public database for product information (no personal data shared)
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (can be disabled in app settings)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all your data
  • Profile Management: Create, edit, or delete profiles at any time
  • Analytics Opt-out: Disable usage analytics in app settings
  • Data Export: Export your scan history and profiles (coming soon)

6. Children's Privacy

SafeEats does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/0V3RR1DE0/SafeEats/issues
  • Email: Contact through GitHub repository
