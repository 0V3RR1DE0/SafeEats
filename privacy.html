Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

SafeEats is designed with complete privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal data whatsoever.

  • Profile Data: Dietary restrictions and allergen preferences are stored only on your device
  • Scan History: Products you scan are stored only on your device
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage statistics or analytics
  • No Crash Reports: We do not collect crash reports or technical data
  • No Network Data: Only product information is fetched from OpenFoodFacts when scanning

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any data, we don't use your information in any way. All data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Provide personalized allergen alerts based on your locally stored dietary restrictions
  • Maintain your scan history locally on your device for easy reference
  • All app functionality works entirely offline (except for fetching product data)

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is our top priority:

  • 100% Local Storage: All personal data is stored only on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Your profiles and scan history never leave your device
  • No Encryption Needed: Since data stays on your device, we don't encrypt it
  • No Servers: We don't have servers that store your personal information
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Impossible since we don't collect any data

4. Third-Party Services

SafeEats only connects to one external service:

  • OpenFoodFacts: Public database for product information (no personal data shared, only product barcodes)
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or any tracking services
  • No Ads: We don't use advertising networks

5. Your Rights and Choices

Since we don't collect data, you have complete control:

  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all your data (since it's only on your device)
  • Profile Management: Create, edit, or delete profiles at any time
  • No Opt-outs Needed: There's nothing to opt out of since we don't collect data
  • Complete Privacy: Your data never leaves your device

6. Children's Privacy

SafeEats is safe for children of all ages since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we don't collect data, changes will likely only be clarifications. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/0V3RR1DE0/SafeEats/issues
  • Email: Contact through GitHub repository
